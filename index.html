CloudSticker

A simple and powerful sticker app for MacOS






1. Simple and effective interface

Cloud Sticker provides an effective and easy way to interact with, allowing you to quickly locate sticky notes from a flurry of applications and providing powerful editing capabilities so you can effectively edit your sticker.





2. Sticker can be saved temporarily

When you want to save the note temporarily, you can store it in a staging area that exists in the MacOS status bar and reopen it from the status bar button when you want to use it again.

3. Work across devices using iCloud

Sync from iPhone to Mac, iPad to Mac, and Mac to Mac!
